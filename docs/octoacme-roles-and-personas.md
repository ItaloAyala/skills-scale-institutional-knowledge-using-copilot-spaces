# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leads ensure proactive risk identification and management from a security and compliance perspective across all project phases. They collaborate with developers, PMs, and project leadership to embed security into processes and deliverables.

### Responsibilities
- Advise on secure design and implementation practices
- Review risk register items related to security and compliance
- Coordinate with developers and PM on security incident handling and response
- Participate in critical code reviews for security implications
- Escalate security risks and compliance concerns through proper channels
- Help define security acceptance criteria for features

### Goals
- Minimize security and compliance risks in project delivery
- Foster a culture of security awareness across the team
- Ensure timely identification and remediation of security gaps

### Typical Communication
- Security-focused code review comments
- Risk escalation during weekly syncs
- Participation in incident response and retrospectives
- Security guidance in design and planning phases

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With Developers**: Reviews PRs for security implications, provides secure coding guidance
- **With Product Manager**: Advises on security requirements and acceptance criteria
- **With Project Manager**: Escalates security risks; participates in risk management updates
- **With Technical Writer**: Ensures security documentation and guidelines are clear and accessible

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers maintain process and product documentation quality and accessibility. They partner with cross-functional teams to ensure clarity, consistency, and completeness of all project and operational documentation.

### Responsibilities
- Create and update project and process documentation
- Ensure version control, clarity, and accessibility of all docs
- Maintain consistent documentation standards and templates
- Participate in sprint planning to identify documentation needs
- Review and refine technical content for clarity
- Collaborate with team members to capture and document processes

### Goals
- Reduce onboarding time through clear, accessible documentation
- Maintain single source of truth for project and process knowledge
- Improve cross-team communication through well-structured documentation

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning participation to identify doc requirements
- Collaboration with Developers and PMs on content accuracy
- Documentation review and iteration feedback
- Updates to process docs and team wikis

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With Developers**: Collaborates on technical documentation and implementation details
- **With Product Manager**: Documents feature specs, acceptance criteria, and release notes
- **With Project Manager**: Updates project charters, timelines, and process documentation
- **With Security Lead**: Documents security guidelines and compliance requirements
- **With UX Designer**: Captures user-facing documentation and help content

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers represent user experience needs and advocate for usability throughout project delivery. They ensure that features are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with user needs and business goals.

### Responsibilities
- Collaborate on design decisions and user-centric acceptance criteria
- Participate in design reviews and user feedback validation
- Advise on user-centric quality standards and usability testing
- Identify and escalate UX risks and usability concerns
- Provide design guidance to support developer implementation
- Ensure accessibility standards are met in deliverables

### Goals
- Deliver user-centric, intuitive features that drive adoption
- Reduce post-release support burden through better UX
- Maintain consistency in user experience across features

### Typical Communication
- Design documentation and mockups shared during planning
- UX-focused code review comments
- Participation in acceptance testing and QA reviews
- User feedback and usability testing insights

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With Product Manager**: Validates feature definitions align with user needs; provides user research insights
- **With Developers**: Reviews implementations for UX consistency; guides design decisions
- **With Project Manager**: Escalates UX risks; participates in sprint planning
- **With QA/Testing**: Collaborates on acceptance criteria and usability test scenarios
- **With Technical Writer**: Ensures user-facing documentation matches design intent

---

## Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Customer Success Managers ensure customer value realization and gather post-release feedback. They serve as a bridge between customers and the product and project teams to drive adoption and inform future improvements.

### Responsibilities
- Coordinate post-release customer communication and training
- Gather and escalate customer feedback to Product and Project teams
- Identify support gaps, training needs, and usability concerns
- Monitor customer adoption and success metrics post-release
- Provide customer perspective input during planning and retrospectives
- Support incident response and customer communication during critical issues

### Goals
- Maximize customer adoption and satisfaction with released features
- Provide customer voice and feedback to drive prioritization
- Reduce support burden through proactive customer enablement

### Typical Communication
- Post-release customer communication and updates
- Feedback summaries during retrospectives and planning
- Participation in release planning for customer readiness
- Monthly/quarterly customer success metrics reviews

### Interaction with Other Roles
- **With Product Manager**: Provides customer feedback to inform backlog prioritization
- **With Project Manager**: Participates in release planning for customer communication strategy; provides input on customer impact in retrospectives
- **With Technical Writer**: Reviews documentation for customer clarity; identifies gaps in help content
- **With Developers**: Escalates customer-reported bugs and usability concerns
- **With QA/Testing**: Participates in acceptance testing from a customer usability perspective
